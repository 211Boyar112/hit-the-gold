Responsible Gaming

At hit-the-gold.com, we promote responsible gaming. This website provides informational content only and may link to third-party operators. Gambling should be treated as entertainment, not as a way to make money.

Last updated:

1) Adults only (18+)

This website is intended for adults aged 18 or older (or the legal gambling age in your jurisdiction). If you are underage, please leave the site.

2) Keep control

  • Set personal limits for time and spending.
  • Only gamble with money you can afford to lose.
  • Do not chase losses.
  • Take regular breaks.

3) Gambling risks

Gambling involves risk and can lead to financial loss. Outcomes are random and no strategy guarantees a win. If gambling stops being enjoyable, it may be time to pause or stop.

4) Warning signs

You may be at risk if you:

  • spend more time or money than planned,
  • gamble to escape stress or problems,
  • feel pressure to recover losses quickly,
  • hide gambling activity from others.

5) Tools offered by operators

Many licensed gambling operators provide responsible gaming tools, such as:

  • deposit and loss limits,
  • session reminders,
  • cool-off or self-exclusion options.

Use these tools where available to stay in control.

6) Get help and support

If you feel gambling is becoming a problem, consider seeking professional help. The following organizations provide information and support:

  • BeGambleAware — gambleaware.org
  • GamCare — gamcare.org.uk
  • GAMSTOP — gamstop.co.uk
  • Local support services in your country or region

7) Our role

We do not offer gambling services and cannot control how third-party operators manage accounts or limits. Always review the responsible gaming policies of any operator you choose to use.

Reminder: Gambling should be a form of entertainment. If it is no longer enjoyable or causes harm, stopping and seeking support is a responsible decision.
